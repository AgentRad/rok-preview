The Ring

Each Key is a working artist.

Casting directors, music directors, producers, and educators use the Ring of Keys directory every day to find queer musical theatre artists across the country. Every profile, self-described.

A short history

From a hashtag in 2014 to a national directory.

It started in 2014 with a viral moment around Fun Home. By 2015 it was a 501(c)(3) built by queer musical theatre artists who needed a way to find each other and be found.

Ten years on, the directory is the largest of its kind.
