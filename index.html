Our story

Started by artists. Run by artists. For artists.

Ring of Keys began as a viral hashtag in 2014 after a moment in Fun Home went unrecognized. By 2015 it was a 501(c)(3), built by queer musical theatre artists who needed a way to find each other and be found.

Ten years on, the directory is the largest of its kind. Casting directors, producers, music directors, and educators use it daily. Every Key sets their own profile, their own pronouns, their own terms.

What we stand on

Visibility, safety, and the work.

Every profile, every event, every resource runs through these. Visibility is not optional in this industry. Safety is not negotiable. The work is the reason we built this.

  • Visibility · Queer artists named, profiled, findable.
  • Safety · Members control their own data, their own outreach.
  • Access · Membership is free. Events are free or low-cost.
  • Community · We hire, refer, and root for each other.
