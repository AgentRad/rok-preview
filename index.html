01 / Mission

We exist so that queer, trans, and gender non-conforming musical theatre artists can be found by the people who want to work with them, and find each other.

Ring of Keys is a 501(c)(3) artist service organization founded in 2015. Membership is free for artists.

03 / Programs

Four programs. One ring.

The Directory
A free, searchable index of queer musical theatre artists used daily by casting directors, music directors, producers, and educators.
Resources
Audition prep, casting guides, mental health, financial wellness, and four seasons of the Key Change podcast.
Events
Monthly Community Nights, casting director panels, craft workshops, and the annual KEY Project residency.
Membership
Free for artists. A profile, members-only resources, RSVP priority, and the casting calls digest.
04 / Community

First Tuesday. Every month. Always free.

Themed gatherings with working artists, casting directors, and chosen family. Some virtual, some in person. Members get RSVP priority.
