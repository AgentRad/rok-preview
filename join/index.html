Become a Key
Membership

Become a Key.

Free for artists. Five-minute signup. A profile in the directory, members-only resources, Community Night access, and casting call notifications.

What you get

A profile, a community, and a working tool.

i.

Directory profile

Headshot, bio, range, pronouns, location, union, links to reel and website. You control every field. You edit any time.

ii.

Members-only resources

Full library access. Rep book builder, headshot photographer list, financial wellness guides, transition resources, and more.

iii.

Community Nights

RSVP priority for monthly Community Nights, members-only conversations, and the annual KEY Project showcase.

iv.

Casting calls

Weekly digest of breakdowns from casting partners actively searching the directory. Optional. Unsubscribe anytime.

Who can join

Self-identified, self-described, self-tagged.

If you are a musical theatre artist, onstage or off, who identifies as a queer woman, transgender, or gender non-conforming, you are eligible. We do not gatekeep identity. You define the terms.

Musical theatre artist includes performers, music directors, directors, choreographers, stage managers, designers, playwrights, composers, lyricists, dramaturgs, and everyone in between.

Membership is free. We do not sell or share member data. Ever.
