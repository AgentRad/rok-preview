The bar

Supplier qualification criteria

Buyers trust PartsPort because every supplier is vetted. To list, your company must meet all six.

Verified business

A registered legal entity with at least two years of trading history and checkable trade references.

Certified & authentic

ISO 9001 or equivalent quality management, and only genuine OEM or authorized-distributor stock — no counterfeits.

Lead-time reliability

Documented lead times met on at least 95% of orders. ETAs are tracked against your real delivery performance.

Quality & returns process

A defined inbound inspection process and a clear, fair return policy for defective or mis-shipped parts.

Capacity & live inventory

Accurate, regularly updated stock levels and the capacity to fulfill repeat and bulk orders.

Insurance & compliance

Current liability insurance and compliance with all regulations covering any regulated or hazardous parts you list.
